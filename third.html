Beautiful Soul in the Cruel World....

-By Sakshi Vashishtha

Under the warm and bright skies,

There was she with tender heart and smiling eyes.

Windows of her soul is open to light,

She always seems happy, bubbly and bright.

 

Her good deeds ,this universe never mentiojned,

But still,vessel of her soul is filled with good intentions.

Always trying to be considerate,

But to push her down, this world is so desperate.

 

She is passionate and has the spark,

But destiny, always trying to put her in the dark.

She's the brightest start in the gloomy night,

But this cruel world left her uptight.

 

The kindness in her eyes doesn't need any explaination,

But the awful acts of the cruel world left her in vexation.

But thank the lord, came into the light,

Her friends and family who made that spark re-ignite.

 

Through all the thick n thins, their bonds will endure,

To see the power of goodness, look at their love so pure.

World say good intentions and modesty take you no where,

See what she has earned, a beautiful circcle I sware.
